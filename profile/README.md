- **Linked Track Editing** – Edit multiple tracks simultaneously in **Ableton Live 11 Suite**.  
- **Macro Variations** – Store and recall macro knob states inside **Ableton Live 11 Suite**.  
- **CV Tools** – Control modular gear from **Ableton Live 11 Suite** without extra hardware.  
- **Instrument Racks** – Layer and split instruments inside **Ableton Live 11 Suite**.  
- **Effect Racks** – Build complex effect chains with **Ableton Live 11 Suite** macro controls.  
- **Max for Live** – Extend **Ableton Live 11 Suite** with thousands of community devices.  
- **Audio to MIDI** – Convert drums, harmony, or melody to MIDI clips inside **Ableton Live 11 Suite**.
